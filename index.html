What is
DSA?

DSA is the foundation of problem-solving in computer science.

01
Data StructuresWays to organize data efficiently (e.g., Arrays, Trees, Graphs).
02
AlgorithmsStep-by-step methods to solve problems (e.g., Sorting, Searching).

MindsetPerspective
The subtle difference

Coder vs.
Engineer

The Coder

Writes scripts to make things work. Focuses on syntax and getting a quick result. Struggles with scalability.

The Software Engineer

Uses DSA to design robust systems. Thinks about edge cases, performance, and scaling before writing a line of code.

The Shift in
Hiring

Old
The 80% GripPreviously, you needed an 80% mastery of raw syntax and algorithms to pass.
New
The 60-70% ShiftNow, the acceptable grip might be 60-70%, BUT problem-solving and architectural logic are more critical than ever.

The EvaluatorCompanies now hire you to evaluate AI's output, not just blindly copy-paste it.
